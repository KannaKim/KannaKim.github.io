<h1>Our Vision</h1>


<!-- # Vision Statement -->

<p>Stalender is a simple and helpful app that brings together a calendar and study tools to help students stay on track and do their best. Our target audience is going to be students who want to study better.
It’s made to make students’ lives easier by being a go-to place for organizing their busy schedules and schoolwork. With Stalender, students can easily add, view, change, or remove events like homework, quizzes, and tests. They can also include their class schedules, daily plans like work, and even time for fun activities or hobbies. The app keeps everything updated automatically, so students don’t have to worry about forgetting anything. Each student has their own profile where they can log in and see their personalized schedule for the day, making it simple to stay organized and focused.
Stalender’s main goal is to save students time and make studying less stressful. It lets students add their test and quiz dates, along with how hard they feel each subject is. Based on this, the app gives simple and helpful suggestions for when to study. For example, it might recommend spending more days preparing for a tough subject or studying earlier in the day when students are more focused. This way, students can plan their study time without overthinking it and still have time for breaks and other activities.
Stalender isn’t just a regular calendar—it’s like a study buddy that makes studying easier and more productive. To help students stay focused, it plays calming music right in the app, so there’s no need to open other apps that might distract them. It also has a timer to help students stick to their study plans while reminding them to take breaks. These breaks are important to recharge, avoid getting too tired, and make study time more effective.
One of the best things about Stalender is its flashcard feature. Students can make their own flashcards for each chapter or subject, helping them quickly go over important topics. These flashcards are personalized, so they focus on exactly what each student needs to study. This makes learning more effective and saves time. Plus, students don’t need to spend extra money on separate flashcard apps—they can do it all in one place with Stalender.
The success of Stalender depends on how much students like using it. If they find it simple, helpful, and enjoyable, they’ll give it good ratings—ideally 4 stars or more out of 5. Seeing at least 50 logins a day would show that students trust Stalender to keep them organized and on track with their studies. Another sign of success is if the app helps students improve their grades by 0.2 points or more, showing it really makes a difference. The main goal is for Stalender to become a part of students’ everyday lives. When they use it regularly to plan their day, keep up with assignments, and make studying easier, it means Stalender is truly helping them do their best.
Stalender is more than just an app—it’s a helpful companion for students, making it easier to stay organized, focused, and stress-free. Whether it’s balancing school, work, or hobbies, Stalender is there every step of the way, simplifying life and making learning more enjoyable.</p>
