# About Us

## Who We Are
We are a dedicated team of students driven by the desire to make time management simpler and more effective for our peers. With firsthand experience navigating the complexities of student life, we've channeled our passion and knowledge into creating a tool that truly supports academic and personal success.

## Why Choose Us?
Our app isn't just another organizational tool—it's built from the ground up with empathy and care. We’ve designed every feature to solve real challenges students face, with a focus on usability, creativity, and reliability. Whether it’s studying smarter, collaborating better, or staying organized, our app is here to empower you.

## A Space to Study and Focus
Our study screen sets the stage for productive work sessions. With the ability to set a timer, students can plan their study time effectively, breaking tasks into manageable sections. Alongside this, carefully curated music enhances focus, helping you to concentrate and stay in the zone. It's more than just a timer—it’s a dedicated space that fosters success.

## Stay Organized with a Smarter Calendar
Our intuitive calendar view provides both monthly and daily perspectives, making it easy to plan ahead. Events are color-coded for quick understanding, ensuring clarity even on your busiest days. Whether it's lectures, study sessions, or important deadlines, the calendar keeps everything in one place for seamless organization.

## Timely Reminders and Notifications
Never miss a beat with our smart notifications! The app sends reminders tailored to your schedule—alerting you to upcoming classes, study sessions, and even test preparation. These helpful nudges ensure you stay on top of your goals while minimizing stress.
